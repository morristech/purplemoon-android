The official Android app for Purplemoon, the Switzerland-based network for LGBT and friends.

Privacy policy

In brief

This application uses the API (application programming interface) of the Purplemoon website to perform actions with your account. This means that it will necessarily exchange information with Purplemoon to perform the app's functions.

This app uses the Google Play Services to provide push notifications, over which the Purplemoon website can send information to the app on your device.

This app does not share your information with any other service or company, and there is no plan at this time to do so at the time of this writing.

Your data

Purplemoon

The app exchanges data with the servers of the Purplemoon website when you use the app. For example: Sent messages and visited profiles. Please see the the the Purplemoon webseite for their privacy policy.

This includes:

  • Credentials to authenticate you to the Purpleoon website
  • Explicitely provided data (uploaded pictures, messages)
  • Your interactions (creating post-its, visiting profiles)
  • Location data, if you enable the feature (Showing users near your location)
  • Data required for internet communication (your IP address)

Google Play Services

Google Play Services are used by the Purplemoon website to send push notifications to your device to notify you of new events - such as messages, profile views and similar. Please see the Google Privacy Policies for further details.
