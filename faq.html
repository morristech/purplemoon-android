Purplemoon for Android

Deutsche Version

The official Android app for Purplemoon, the Switzerland-based network for LGBT and friends.

FAQ - frequently asked questions

General

Do the developers earn money with the app?

No, the app is distributed for free, there are no ads and we don't get paid for it.

Will it be ad-free forever?

We're not planning any ads at this time. We can't say never: If the developers are suddenly charged significant fees for distribution of this app, we may need to implement ads to pay those.

I found a bug!

Please see the list of known issues if it was already reported, then report a new one.

Install / uninstall

The app does not work on my device!

We're trying to support as many devices and versions of Android as possible. Please file a bug describing exactly which device you own and which version of Android it runs.

Does the app run on unofficial versions ("Custom-ROM") of Android?

Probably, if you have the Google Play services installed. But we can't offer any guarantees.

What are the minimum requirements for installation?

The mimimum requirements are always enforced by the Play Store. Besides a compatible Android version, the Google Play Services are required for push notifications.

Your data

How is my data used?

The app exchanges data with the servers of the Purplemoon website when you use the app. For example: Sent messages and visited profiles.

This includes:

  • Explicitely provided data (uploaded pictures, messages)
  • Your interactions (creating post-its, visiting profiles)
  • Location data, if you enable the feature (Showing users near your location)
  • Data required for internet communication (your IP address)

This app does not send your data to other websites apart from the Purplemoon website.

The privacy policy of the Purplemoon webseite informs you how they use your data.

Power User

What is the power user program?

The Purplemoon website offers some additional features to paying users to pay its bills. To see details, check out the "support us" page of Purplemoon.

Do you earn anything from the power user program?

No, the developers of the app are not paid and don't get a share of any Purplemoon revenue. Given some fees, this app is developed at a loss.

Why is feature X only available for power users?

This is determined by the application interface of Purplemoon and determined by the company running the Purplemoon website.

How can I become a power user?

Please see the "support us" page of Purplemoon.
